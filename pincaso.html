Christmas Magazines


Company: Pincaso S.A.S
Year: 2017

While working in Pincaso I was in charge of the publicity of the new christmas collections. With the product, the marketing team, interori designers and I created "christmas living rooms" by gathering together products that we could pair together to create different collections. Each collection had a theme and a name to go with it. With this idea, we were able to sell bundles of items much faster. The spaces were put together by the team of interior designers, the photography was done by the marketing manager and I; and the final design of the magazine was made by me. After the success of the magazine, we were aproached by a famous chain of supermarket La 14, to make their christmas magazine.
