Valeria Micolta

Cali, Colombia

I am a 24 year old Interactive Media Designer from the beautiful and sunny city of Cali, Colombia. Growing up I never thought about studying design, I had other thoughts in mind. I was always a doctor, a lawyer, a nurse, everything but anything related to design. When I finally reached my senior year of high school, things became blurry, I didn't resonate with any of the options I had at hand and the process of choosing my career was long and difficult, but I finally found my calling. I started to love design and I enjoy letting my imagination run wild and giving myself new challenges to overcome. Editorial shots and fashion photography became my passion throughout my university years and I would make up an excuse to photograph anyone. I like publicity and marketing projects because they get me thinking of new ideas to approach the public, working with brands to build their image.


I became interested in interactive installation and have been studying about them for a few years now. It brings me great joy to create experiences and to have the opportunity to build projects that can feedback emotions and contribute to a much visually pleasing world. Aside from design, traveling has become a big passion of mine, it's helped me get a better glimpse of the world and I learn something new from every place that I visit, teachings which I can later on put into my work. I love to dance and, ofcourse, are you really a traveler if you aren't a big foodie?
