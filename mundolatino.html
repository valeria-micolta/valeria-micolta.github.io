[ Web Design ]
Internship in New Delhi, India
Role: Graphic Designer
2017


Mundo Latino is a Latin American center in the heart of india, New Delhi. They thrive to teach students of all ages and genders about the Latin American culture, the language, the dance, the food and the customs. The current website design is not in focus with the objective nor the ambiance of the school currently, so the idea behind the new design was to make a site that would encourage future students to know more about the institute, as well as some cultural fact about different Latin American countries to encourage them to make their decision.

Whilst brainstorming about the site and its contents, we knew the site had to give that cheerful atmosphere the institute has. The whole objective of it was that, it needed to be welcoming and cheerful. For this, we decided to go with a color palette that would reflect just that, a fresh set of colors to show joy, and also to be able to gain the interest of future students, in the sense that they'd want to keep browsing and learning more about the institute.
